Workout Logbook (Personal Trainer Edition)

As a personal trainer, you are expected to track your clients progress and log everything they do for future reference. This helps ensure progress is being made and allows trainers to reflect on previous programs to understand their clients responses to the workouts they are programming. I integraded my knowledge from personal training and iOS development to create a virtual logbook where you can easily create specialized workout plans for multiple clients while the app automatically creates weight tracking charts and displays all of the information in an organized manner.
