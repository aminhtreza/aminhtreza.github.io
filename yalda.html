Winter solstice

Longest night of the year! Yalda marks the winter solstice—literally the darkest and longest night of the year (by 1 minute lol). Why do we celebrate the extra minute? Because every single minute with loved ones is worth celebrating.

Pomegranates and Watermelons

Two iconic fruits of Yalda are pomegranates and watermelons. Pomegranate seeds’ bright red color represents the glow of dawn and the glow of life; watermelons are believed to keep you healthy and safe from illness in the coming winter months.

Fal E Hafez

Hafez Fortune-Telling; Persians open a Divan of Hafez at random and interpret the verse they land on as a form of fortune-telling. The beloved 14th-century poet is personally speaking to you across the centuries and providing his wisdom and insights. Yes he was Shirazi (we da best)
